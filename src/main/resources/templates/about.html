About Greenhouse Coffee

Our Story

Greenhouse Coffee was founded in 2000 with a simple mission: to bring the best coffee machines and parts to coffee lovers everywhere.

Our Products

We offer five types of coffee machines, from our simple Greenhouse Mini which is perfect for household use and quick brews, to our Greenhouse Signature which is ideal for cafes and restaurants. We also provide a selection of parts for maintenance and repair.

We believe it is important to offer affordable products, while also providing excellent customer service. This is why we have priced our goods so competitively.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing the highest quality products and the best customer service in the industry. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
